# Tool Photo Labeler 🛢️

**Because somebody screwed up and now we all gotta take pictures.**

Look, we ain't here to waste time. You got tools going in a hole, you need pictures, and you need those pictures labeled before somebody loses their mind at 2am on location. That's what this does. Simple as that.

---

## What It Does

Takes your tool photos and burns a label right on 'em. Well name, tool name, stage number, timestamp — all of it. One image. Done. No more texting mystery photos to the company man wondering what the hell he's looking at.

You want a collage? It'll make you a collage. Five photos, one image, numbered 1 of 5 through 5 of 5 like a professional operation.

---

## Features

- **Saved Wells** — type your well names in once, tap 'em forever
- **Single Photo** mode for when you just need one shot labeled
- **Batch Collage** mode for when you got a full BHA to document
- **Stage Number dial** — holds your last stage so you ain't retyping it every time
- **Timestamp** baked right in
- **Your colors** — pick 'em, save 'em, they don't go anywhere
- **Everything sticks** — mode, position, font size, all of it. Set it and forget it

## How To Use It

**First time setup:**
1. Open **https://jaredfiverniner.github.io/tool-labeler** on your phone
2. Tap the share icon in your browser
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it whatever you want and tap Add
5. Now it's sitting on your home screen like a real app — tap it to open it anytime

**Adding your wells:**
1. Open the Saved Wells section
2. Type your well name and tap **+ ADD**
3. Do that for all your wells — they save permanently

**Labeling photos:**
1. Tap your well name to auto-fill the label
2. Pick **Single Photo** or **Batch Collage** mode
3. Add your photo(s)
4. Hit Save
5. Image opens in a new tab — tap the share icon and save to your Photos

**Send it to your crew:**
Text them the link. They open it, add it to their home screen, done. No app store, no login, no BS.
